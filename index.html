Join us for our wedding

January 10th, 2016 in Coachella, California

Meet us in Coachella

We'll be having our wedding at the beautiful Corona Yacht Club.

The sun will be setting early so pack a light coat to keep partying into the night.

Order of Ceremonies

Ceremony - 3:00pm

Cocktail Hour - 4:00pm

Dinner and Dancing - 5:00pm

Where to stay

Fantasy Springs Resort and Casino

Most guests are staying at the Fantasy Springs Resort and Casino. Rooms for Sunday night are still available online and shuttle service to the venue will be provided.

Transportation

Shuttles

We will be providing shuttles from the Fantasy Springs Resort and Casino to the Corona Yacht Club starting at 2:00pm. The shuttles will return to the Casino starting at 10:00pm.

Lyft

Download the Lyft rideshare app for a ride to the venue. First time riders can use code KIMBERLY20 for a free first ride.

Wedding Registries

Thank you for coming to our wedding. We hope everyone has a great time celebrating with our friends and family.

If you'd like to get a gift, we're registered at

Honey Fund Crate and Barrel Bed Bath & Beyond

Questions?

No problem, email us at wedding@kimandkev.in.
